Applied Research Laboratories is a dynamic R&D organization of The University of Texas at Austin, and a leader in R&D for advanced signal processing algorithms and underwater sonar systems.

As a university laboratory, ARL:UT is committed to excellence in its contributions to education, research and public service. ARL:UT is constantly looking for students in fields of engineering, mathematics, computer science and physics. Job title and rate of pay will be commensurate with the qualifications of the person selected. Job positions are located in northwest Austin. All positions are security sensitive; conviction verifications will be conducted on the applicants selected. U.S. Citizenship required.

STUDENT RESEARCH ASSISTANT positions available in the Sonar Development Division.

Help is needed for analog and digital circuit design and testing, FPGA/CPLD development, C/C++ software development, embedded system development, real-time processing, and multiprocessor programming.

For information e-mail Greg Allen, or call 835-3487.

For additional information about ARL:UT, visit our web page at: (http://www.arlut.utexas.edu)
