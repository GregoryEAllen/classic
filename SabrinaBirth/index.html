It's A Girl!

Sabrina Fair Llorens Allen

born Friday, May 30, 1997 at 12:30 PM

7 pounds, 2.4 ounces; 20 inches long

Sabrina was born with lots of blonde hair, and dark blue (almost navy) eyes.

Sabrina didn't handle mommy's contractions very well, so the doctor decided it would be safest to take her out early. Labor was induced at 6AM, and 6.5 hours later we had a baby girl! She is not a small baby -- just imagine how big she would be if she had gotten several more weeks of growth!

We delivered at Seton Northwest Hospital.

Naming Sabrina

Call us saps, but the first movie we saw together was Sabrina, starring Harrison Ford and Julia Ormond. We also played music from the soundtrack at our wedding.

In the movie, Sabrina recites the poem after which she is named:

Sabrina fair
    Listen where thou art sitting
Under the glassy, cool, translucent wave,
    In twisted braids of lilies knitting
The loose train of thy amber-dropping hair;
    Listen for dear honour's sake,
    Goddess of the silver lake,
      Listen and save.
- John Milton: Comus (c.1637)

(Besides, it was one of the few names we could both agree upon :-)
