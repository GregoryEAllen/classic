Greg Allen's Home Page

I am a Senior Engineering Scientist in the Enabling Capabilities Division of the Advanced Technology Laboratory at Applied Research Laboratories, The University of Texas at Austin. I have previously been a member of both the Special Warfare Division and the Sonar Development Division. I received my Ph.D. in the UT Electrical and Computer Engineering Department under the supervision of Professor Brian Evans.

Software Releases

  • MCSB 2.0.0 [documentation], the Multi-Client Shared Buffer, a high-throughput many-to-many message-based middleware for implementing soft real-time systems.
  • CPN 1.0.2 [documentation], Computational Process Networks, a model and a framework library for real-time high-throughput streaming signal and image processing. Also my dissertation topic.
  • LibVariant 1.0.1 [documentation], scripting-language-like variables (Variant) in C++, with serialization, schemas, and argument parsing. Formerly a component of the CPN library.
