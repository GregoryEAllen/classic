Greg Allen's Home Page

I am a Senior Engineering Scientist in the Sonar Development Division of the Advanced Technology Laboratory at Applied Research Laboratories, The University of Texas at Austin. I received my Ph.D. in the UT Electrical and Computer Engineering Department under the supervision of Professor Brian Evans.
Sonar Development Division is looking for students, both graduate and undergraduate

Software Releases

  • MCSB 2.0.0 [documentation], the Multi-Client Shared Buffer, a high-throughput many-to-many message-based middleware for implementing soft real-time systems.
  • CPN 1.0.2 [documentation], Computational Process Networks, a model and a framework library for real-time high-throughput streaming signal and image processing. Also my dissertation topic.
  • LibVariant 1.0.0 [documentation], scripting-language-like variables (Variant) in C++, with serialization, schemas, and argument parsing. Formerly a component of the CPN library.
